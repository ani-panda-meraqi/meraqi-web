Cookie Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how AiOne LLC ("AiOne," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our websites and the Meraqi platform (the "Service"). It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function, remember your preferences, and understand how the site is used. We also use related technologies such as local storage and pixels; we refer to all of these as "cookies" here.

2. Categories we use

3. Your choices

Managing consent. Where required by law, we ask for your consent to non-essential cookies through a consent banner, and you can change your choices at any time via the cookie settings link in the site footer.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings, including blocking or deleting them. Blocking strictly necessary cookies may break parts of the Service. Many browsers support a "Do Not Track" or Global Privacy Control signal; we honor recognized opt-out signals where required by law.

4. Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be set by service providers acting on our behalf (for example, analytics or payment providers). Their use of information is governed by their own policies. We do not permit third parties to use cookies to access text-messaging opt-in data or consent.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new effective date.
